About US

Chingkee personally trained in
Taiwan about the ways of making milktea;
and all our recipes, drinks, and ingredients
are straight out of Taiwan,
making sure that what you get at
Chingkeetea is authentic Tawainese milktea.

Here at Chingkeetea, we want to make sure that our teas give a little spark to your day. Each of our branches tell different stories, and we want to share that feeling of creativity and freedom to you.
